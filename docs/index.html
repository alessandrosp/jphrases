JPhrases

What's this all about?

JPhrases is a gamified tool to improve your Japanese by practicing with phrases rather than individual kanji or words. It's rather simple: try to translate each phrase in your head, when you're ready click on Show the r(e)sults. and get the correct answer plus additional information about the various elements in the sentence.


Units

Each unit has around 12 phrases for you to pratice. Once you've mastered all the sentences in a unit you can move to the next one. Note that the unit numbers (e.g., Unit 1) could change with time as more units are added.
